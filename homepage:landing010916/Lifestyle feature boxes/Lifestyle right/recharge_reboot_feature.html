RECHARGE, REBOOT

The streets are awash with stretch-lycra and working-out is now an essential part of our weekly routine – we just can’t get enough of those endorphins. So why let it all slip when you skip the city? A fitness retreat is the best way to immerse yourself in a new sport or focus on fine-tuning your practice. So whether you’re looking to strengthen your sun salutations or firm up that forehand, here are four reasons to get packing.
 
 

Yoga Haven

Swap your cramped class for the sweeping, lush-green landscapes of north India, where a stone’s throw from Rishikesh – the birthplace of yoga and The Beatles’ mediation guru Marharshi Mahesh Yogi – Ananda Spa awaits. Fine-tune your practice with a tailor-made program of traditional yoga classes in the manicured grounds of the Marharaja’s Palace overlooking the Ganges river valley, sound-tracked by frolicking families of monkeys. And as for soothing stretched limbs - the 24,000 sq-ft spa offers over 80 treatments, from Ayurveduic therapies to Western spa rituals.
 
 
 
 
CREDIT: Ananda Spa, India (www.anandaspa.com)

Surf Escape

The pristine beaches of Dominical, south Costa Rica, are home to Kalon Surf School. Founded by an ex-investment banker and her husband, it offers a week long program of intensive tuition and boasts motivation-boosting luxury lodgings, gourmet food, legendary smoothies, fresh aloe vera from their own gardens to soothe sun and salt exposed skin and a mid-week complimentary message. With everything well and truly taken care of, riding the pumped-up tides will remain your primary focus with a team of trained experts dedicated to pushing your workout to the next level.
 
 
 
 
CREDIT: Kalon Surf School, Costa Rica (www.kalonsurf.com)

Trekking Adventure

Set in the Danum Valley conservation area where the trees are the oldest, tallest and most dense, Borneo Rainforest Lodge’s trekking program enlists knowledgeable guides to venture into seldom-seen places beneath the jungle’s canopy. Besides invigorating excursions to the Kadazan-Duusun tribal burial cliff and up the 26-metre high canopy walkway this eco-lodge, with its impeccable green credentials, is an extraordinary feast for the senses. After a day exploring the network of natural trails switch off all devices and drift off to the sounds of a cornucopia of exotic wildlife in one of 31 chalets.
 
 
 
CREDIT: Borneo Rainforest Lodge, Danum Valley (www.borneonaturetours.com)

Tennis Getaway

Inspired by Wimbledon’s white-clad athletes? Take your racket on tour to Mallorca’s La Residencia and perfect your serve on one of the hotel’s two immaculate tennis courts in the midst of the Tramuntana mountains. Look to resident professional Toni Canals for tips on how to ace those tricky volley shots and once well-practiced, sign up to one of the frequent tournaments. Partner flagging? Head for the practice hitting wall or to the pro shop to ponder a new racket, followed by a post work-out stroll through the hotel’s olive groves in the warm Mallorcan sun.
 
 
 
 
CREDIT: Belmond La Residencia, Mallorca (www.belmond.com)
