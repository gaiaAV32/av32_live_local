5 MINUTES WITH
PERSEVERANCE LONDON

 

Meet the man remixing pretty femininity

 
The secret’s out: Perseverance London is the new go-to for ultra-pretty partywear and sweet staples. Tapping into fashion’s romantic mood with his own tantalising twists, Spanish designer Eudald Luis is freshening up the unapologetic femininity. We caught up with Luis to discuss his breakthrough AW16 collection.

Describe the Perseverance London aesthetic.

 
A contrast between feminine and masculine, balanced with updated fabrications and a mix of vintage and modern influences.
 

What’s the story behind the name?

 
I was searching for an impactful and appealing term and Perseverance London stood out. Whether it’s fashion or in life in general, you have to persevere and because of my trajectory I found it really personal.
 
 

Talk us through your signature shapes, fabrics and details?

 
I love to design dresses, especially gowns. I love the shape simplicity of the AW16 gold maxi gown balanced by the bold gold embroidered fabric. The 3D paisley fabric is one of my favourites and I had never seen anything like it. It was the first one I picked for this season.
 

What construction techniques do you swear by?

 
I try to French seam and bind all internal seams to make garments look immaculate on the inside and outside. I also pay a lot of attention to the linings.

How does your Spanish heritage and East London location influence your work?

My temperament is very Spanish and living in East London has a big influence. There’s not a single day that I walk down the street and don’t see someone that catches my eye with a very strong sense of style and attitude.
 

As romance and lace are currently in vogue-how do you retain your USP?

I tend to use the natural eyelash finishing with lace and play with it. Every season you will see a true evolution that rides out current trends but also injects something new that is wearable and eye-catching.
