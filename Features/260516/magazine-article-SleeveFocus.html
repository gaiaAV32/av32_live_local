STYLING STATEMENT SLEEVES

7 days a week

 

When basking in the reflected glory of this season’s statement sleeves, it’s easy to wonder how we managed without them. Their experimental volume and imaginative embellishments has elevated tops, dresses and the like to must-have-now status. Frankly we’re spoilt for choice. There’s one for every day of the week and just to be sure, we took it upon ourselves to put some of these new proportions into perspective below...

 
 

 
 
 
 

 
MONDAY

Start the week stylishly in 3.1 Phillip Lim’s deconstructed shirt dress as it means business. The knots add an edge to the elegance, imbuing the trailing sleeves with a cool, urban attitude.


3.1 PHILLIP LIM White Knot Sleeve Shirt Dress

 
 

 
 
 

 
TUESDAY

Victoria Victoria Beckham’s zingy blouse nips in waists and excels with tailoring and denim. The pièce de résistance is the folded kimono sleeve which adds an ingenious, elbow framing flourish.


VICTORIA VICTORIA BECKHAM
Marigold Silk Crepe Gathered Blouse

 

 
 
 
 

 
WEDNESDAY

Ruffling sleeves isn’t new but the 3-D trim is lined in black here to really set the striking strawberry print off. You’ll go from desk to dinner without missing a sartorial beat.


VICTORIA VICTORIA BECKHAM
Ivory Flocked Strawberry Ruffled Blouse

 
 

 
 
 

 
THURSDAY

Lorenzo Serafini uses the idyllic innocence of a billowing sleeve to temper the translucency of this frilled Philosophy button-down. The captivating effect is girly yet grown-up, demure but delightful.


PHILOSOPHY DI LORENZO SERAFINI
Black Silk Floral Frill Blouse

 

 
 
 
 

 
FRIDAY

Roksanda blows up her blue bell sleeves to sculptural extremes then balances them expertly with a sleek torso. Let them shine by keeping all accompaniments.


ROKSANDA Sky Blue Cotton Phoebe Top

 
 

 
 
 

 
WEEKEND

Antonio Berardi creates the ultimate summer occasion top by applying his failsafe flamboyance to the sleeve. The fluted fluidity of the soft trumpet shape keeps the bold look breezy.


ANTONIO BERARDI Pale Pink Camicia Top
