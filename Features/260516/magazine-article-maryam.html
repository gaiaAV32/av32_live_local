ONE STEP AHEAD

Maryam Nassir Zadeh’s It shoes have arrived

 

It’s official: Maryam Nassir Zadeh’s shoes have graduated from cult favourite to full-blown phenomenon. Not unheard of for shoes, granted, except Nassir Zadeh’s designs have earned their ‘it’ status without relying on flashy adornments or ridiculous heels, but let their thoughtful detailing and perfect proportions make a statement. Her minimal mules and simple pumps resonate with the kind of woman who – like Nassir Zadeh – likes her clothes to say something but not speak for her. Thoughtfully designed and undeniably cool, these innately elegant and retro-tinged shoes really will slot chicly into every wardrobe.

 

 

The Maryam Pump and Penelope Mule

 

When Nassir Zadeh launched her eponymous label in 2013, it quickly became a go-to for the kind of intelligent women who treasure style over trends. Drawing on her own impeccable eclectic-yet-minimal style and a refreshingly pragmatic approach to fashion, Nassir Zadeh excels in rethinking classic shoes you already love, updating them with an unexpected heel height or an unusual colour, note the stacked Penelope mules and the heeled Maryam pumps, for instance. Effortless yet elegant, so-now yet timeless, smart yet low-key, is it any wonder these quiet styles are seriously worth shouting about?
