SKIRTS TO SIZZLE IN

Summer minis, midis and maxis

 

Sun comes out, legs go on show, you know how it goes. No wonder then that skirts are a summertime essential, right up there with SPF and a pedi. Long or short, minimal or maxi, dreamily romantic or vibrantly bohemian, it’s simply a matter of taste and temperature.

 



 

Vika Gazinskaya specialises in playful cinematic forms that exude mouth-watering glamour. Note how the waist-whittling fit and juicy jacquard print of this full skirt help it transition from work to weekend and wedding.

 

 



 

Isa Arfen’s tiered ruffles are rhythmic rather than romantic running flamenco-like across this statement skirt. Fitted but fluid with flashes of calf, the monochrome gingham check is a witty twist on tradition.

 

 



 

No skirt style dresses up and down as effortlessly as the pleated midi. Tome’s cornflower blue tone feels fresh and is trending for SS16.

 



 

Jourden’s floor-sweeping skirt might be dramatic but even with its tinsel texture, fringing and contrasting panels, there’s nothing try-hard about it. Tuck in a t-shirt to nail an insouciant vibe.

 



 

Still searching for the ultimate suede mini? Isabel Marant Étoile has surely cracked the code here. Perfect proportions plus distinctive yet understated details, and a dose of bohemian attitude, set it timelessly apart.
