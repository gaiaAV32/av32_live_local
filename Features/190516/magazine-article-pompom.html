IN DETAIL: POMPOMS AND TASSELS

 

The power of the pompom shouldn’t be underestimated. Besides the fact that the materials used to make them have never been finer, the sense of humour pompoms project is infectious. Consider yourself more of a tassels kind of girl? Their textured effect is just as joyful, which makes adopting both adornments, on your accessories at the very least, a no-brainer. We’ve zoomed in on a few of our favourites below.

 

Elina Linardaki

Elina Linardaki really piles on the pompoms (and charms) to create free-spirited and incredibly versatile sandals that the style set can’t get enough of. Inspired by her globetrotting adventures, they look great with everything: dresses, bikinis, even jeans.

 

ELINA LINARDAKI

Penny Lane in Multi

 
 

Muzungu Sisters

The upgraded basket bags that artisans handcraft for the Muzungu Sisters are sustainable and – adorned with both tassels and pom poms – do their bit for the bohemian’s ‘more is more’ mantra. These contrasting textures and bold colours attract even more attention with the gold hardware. (Coming Soon)

 

MUZUNGU SISTERS

Sicilian Basket 2 in Blue

 
 

Dodo Bar Or

Israeli designer Dorit Bar Or, of Dodo Bar Or, is partial to a tasselled trim, using them as a whimsical accent to the cotton jacquards of her home-or-holiday heroes, like this rippling maxi.

 

Dodo Bar Or

Cotton Jacquard Amal Dress

 
 

MINNA PARIKKA

White Low Top Bunny Sneakers

Minna Parikka

Rabbit ears decorate Minna Parikka’s sneakers so given the placement of her pompoms, doesn’t it make sense to think of them as the tails? These white lace ups are much more light-hearted than their counterparts with hints of pastel pink.
