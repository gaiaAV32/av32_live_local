Jonathan Simkhai’s New York

 

With the likes of Gigi Hadid and Emily Ratajkowski hanging on his every seam, and a CFDA/Vogue Fashion Fund win under his belt, it’s clear Jonathan Simkhai is seriously hot property. Carving a niche with his sexy-sporty silhouettes and wonderful fabric manipulation (the second-skin intarsia knits and graphic laces are a house signature), the native New Yorker translates the city’s powerful confidence into his youthful yet sophisticated collections. We asked Simkhai to give us a glimpse of some of his Manhattan hot spots.

Home is... The West Village.
Describe NY in three words: Vibrant, diverse, magnetic.
Best place for a nightcap: Soho House.
Dream Sunday in the city: Waking up early and getting in a great workout, then spending the day outside with friends.
Where do you go when you want to stay out longer than you should? The clubs in Chelsea.
Best coffee: Stumptown in the West Village.
New York style is... Eclectic and inspiring.
Subway or taxi? Uber.
Favorite thing about NY? The people.v And the worst? The winter.

 

Favorite spot for brunch and what would you order?

Cafe Cluny for a Bloody Mary and an omelette.

David Zwirner Gallery, NYC

Where’s worth the hype? Indochine. Still a classic.
What’s your favorite time of year in the city? Early summer.
Must-visit gallery: David Zwirner.
Where do you head when you want to be inspired?Upstate, into the county.
What song/book sums up NY to you? Humans of New York: Stories by Brandon Stanton, a collection of 400 photos of different New Yorkers and their stories.
Top tip to a tourist visiting NY: Don’t forget about Brooklyn while you are here; there’s so much going on in the food and music scene, it’s worth the trip over the bridge.
