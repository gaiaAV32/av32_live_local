NEW ARRIVAL: SEA NY

 

Bringing boho bang up to date with artful twists and supreme wearbility, Sea NY is the New York name to know now. Designers Monica Paolini and Sean Monahan elevate their simple shapes with assorted embellishments to sew up day-to-night dressing. None of boho’s traditional effortlessness and irreverence is lost and as a result, Sea NY’s overall mood is also cool and forward thinking.

The Pre Fall 16 pieces showcase exactly why Sea Ny’s momentum is growing. The folksy dresses can switch dresscodes instantly and have the kind of intricate embroidery and prints that are best appreciated up close. The flattering tops also have dynamic details: clashing textures, fine fringing and tassels to revamp your favourite denim and tailoring elegantly and progressively. All in all, it’s everything you need for an ultra-stylish summer.
