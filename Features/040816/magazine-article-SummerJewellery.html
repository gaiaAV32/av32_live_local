NEW STAPLES TO SNAP UP

 

You wouldn’t build your house on weak foundations so why your wardrobe? A selection of strong, multi-functional staples will cement any line up, making ‘I don’t know what to wear’ moments a thing of the past. Start with tops that can transition from ‘weekend’ to ‘work’ and a few wear everywhere dresses won’t hurt either. Don’t shy away from colour or embellishment either or you’ll be missing some serious styling tricks. Here are four of our favourites.
