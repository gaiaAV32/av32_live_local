ISABEL MARANT ÉTOILE

The secret to effortless Parisian style

 

Isabel Marant is often credited as a force behind boho’s cooler revival. In her arsenal? Slouchy boots and frou-frou minis, embroidered tunics and chunky knits. The French designer brings the same haute bohemian aesthetic inflected with an insouciant ‘I just threw this on’ spirit to her sister line, Étoile. Known for elegant, everyday staples, Étoile exudes the same charming ‘je ne sais quoi’ as the mainline.

 

Military precision: A suede mini and army-style shirt is at once tough and tailored. Consider this your summer uniform.
