PASKAL PINK

 

Ukraine’s name to know

 

Blush Pink Laser-Cut Dress, Blush Pink Crepe Scaled Jacket and A-line Skirt

 
 
 

If you’re thinking pink this season, but prefer the less pretty, more progressive kind, Paskal’s SS16 collection ticks that box and more. Scalpel sharp classic cuts, rows of layered and perforated circles and a smattering of neon origami pompoms create a selection of fresh and original occasionwear. The blush palette adds romance, softening the decorative minimalism and signature laser cuts that the Ukrainian brand is known for, while skin peeks playfully through adding another alluring feature. Architecture graduate turned designer Julia Paskal drew inspiration from beaches of her hometown Odessa and the naked body, and as you’ve probably guessed, shape and structure is vital to her authentic aesthetic. Part of the influx of East European fashion talent making waves in the West, the mother of two only launched her eponymous luxury brand in 2011 and it was shortlisted for the LVMH prize in 2014. With everything proudly made in Ukraine then shown in Kiev and Paris, Paskal’s mainstream momentum is only getting stronger.
