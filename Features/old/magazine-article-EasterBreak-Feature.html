WHAT TO PACK

The travel essentials for weekend getaways.

Whether you celebrate Easter or not, the bank holiday weekend is the perfect excuse for a quick getaway. All you need are a few failsafe favourites to mix and match and you’re good to go.

Sunny Break

These bolts of blue and playful trims will ensure you’re tastefully on trend from beach, to boat, to bar and anywhere in between. They really pop against tan (yours and your accessories).

 

Country Retreat

All of these styles are smartly casual and super versatile in their own right, even those tan mules, so they’ll work wonders at social gatherings when you don’t want to try too hard.

 

City Break

Fingers crossed it will be mild enough to rock this sleeveless knit and skirt combo as shown in your chosen city. If not, a little layering (on top or underneath) goes a long way.

Apres Ski

These hardworking heroes ensure you’ll stay comfortably chic after a long day on the slopes. Flattering denim is a must but note the bold details of the shirt and accessories that effortlessly elevate it.
