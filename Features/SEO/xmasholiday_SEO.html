THE AVENUE 32 HOLIDAY HOT LIST

What to do this Christmas and the designer fashion to wear there

 

Every holiday season brings a whole host of festivities to dress up for. Beyond Christmas dinners and cocktail parties, there’s the ice-skating, carol concerts and annual visit to Father Christmas (aka Santa Claus) among traditional outings with loved ones. Not to mention all-important Christmas Day and New Year’s Eve outfits which require impressive but ultimately comfortable pieces. That’s where Avenue 32 comes in, to help you eliminate ‘I don’t know what to wear’ moments during this busy period with standout designer clothing and accessories you’ll wear for seasons to come. With our exciting edit of fashion designers and brands at your disposal, it’s sure to be your most stylish Christmas yet.
