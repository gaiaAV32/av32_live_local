No.21’s new season designs demand a close-up

 
Designer Alessandro Dell’Acqua’s No. 21 encapsulates both his Italian sense of extravagant, slightly off-beat femininity and the nation’s expert craftsmanship. Taking its name from his birthday and lucky number, the label’s shoes – which give classic and must-have shapes an opulent upgrade – have quickly become a style set favourite. If your feet are already familiar with the label’s signature half-tied, boudoir-esque bow there are new developments this season, as Dell’Acqua turns his attention to the staple ankle boot. Glittering heels, ruffles and miniature buckles result in the perfect combination of timelessness and contemporary flair - also the easiest way to transform jeans and a jumper.
