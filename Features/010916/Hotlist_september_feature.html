Book This

Lisbon, Portugal
It’s back-to-school season – summer holidays are sadly in the past and Christmas still far off. Book yourself a city escape for next month– Lisbon will be a little cooler in the autumn, all the better for exploring. Stay at Palacio Ramalhete, stroll along the seafront and feast on fresh seafood.

Make This

Here’s hoping for an Indian summer and the continued delights of an Aperol Spritz. Ohso- superior to rosé and the Instagrammer’s aperitif of choice, it’s as easy as 3, 2, 1: that’s 3 parts prosecco, 2 parts Aperol and 1 dash of soda. Add ice, a slice of orange and a few mint leaves if you’re feeling fancy.
