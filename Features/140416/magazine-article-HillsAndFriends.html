BAGS OF FUN

 

Hill & Friends and Sonia by Sonia Rykiel.

 

 

If eyes are the window to the soul, then consider bags the sartorial equivalent. Whether you prefer roomier, multitasking styles or can whittle down your essentials into a mini, don’t forget the fun factor. It’s anything about a bag that instantly lifts your look and mood, like a bright pop of colour or an adorable accent. Hill & Friends and Sonia by Sonia Rykiel have had plenty of fun with their SS16 styles, on top of the premium materials and excellent craftsmanship, to maximise their undeniable charm(s).
