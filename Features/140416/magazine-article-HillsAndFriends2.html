BAGS OF FUN

 

Hill & Friends and Sonia Rykiel.

 

 

If eyes are the window to the soul, then consider bags the sartorial equivalent. Whether you prefer roomier, multitasking styles or can whittle down your essentials into a mini, don’t forget the fun factor. It’s anything about a bag that instantly lifts your look and mood, like a bright pop of colour or an adorable accent. Hill & Friends and Sonia Rykiel have had plenty of fun with their SS16 styles, on top of the premium materials and excellent craftsmanship, to really maximise their undeniable charm(s). Scroll down to see them in action.

 

 

Hill & Friends

 
 

Hill & Friends Happy Factory video 2016

 

This film recreates the infectious fun of the unforgettably pink presentation the brand treated Anna Wintour and other industry insiders to at Claridge’s. We’re taken behind the scenes of the ‘Happy Factory’ where the bags are made and fastened with the signature wink smiley face lock. Watch out for Founder and Ex Mulberry Director Emma Hill’s cameo and all the adorable animals.

Sonia Rykiel

 

 

Sonia Rykiel Quilted Leather Mini Bag

 

Sonia herself once said ‘La beauté sera toujours rayée’ which translates as ‘Stripes will always be beautiful” which sums up the iconic fashion house’s preoccupation with the perennial pattern. It’s boldly quilted on bags this season while elsewhere fringing flows full and free, emphasising the brand’s witty sense of ‘70s glamour.
