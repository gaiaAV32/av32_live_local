ANCIENT GREEK SANDALS X PETER PILOTTO

 

An unmissable collaboration.

 

 

Ancient Greek Sandals and Peter Pilotto’s collaboration this season does what the best partnerships do, complements through its contrasts. Taking gladiator sandals into tantalising new territory, the collection showcases the best of both brands’ distinctive aesthetics, and remains true to both. Ancient Greek Sandals founders Christina Martini and Nikolas Minoglou ensured their luxury leather sandals were timelessly handcrafted as usual but left the decorative details to Peter Pilotto and Christopher de Vos. And the innovative duo didn’t disappoint, keeping colours classic and updating them with the elegant-yet-experimental embellishments they’ve become renowned for. Here’s how to wear them.
