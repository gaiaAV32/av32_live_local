All Tied Up

Lace-up shoes to love.

Is there any mood the shoelace doesn’t tap into this season? Sporty, sexy, preppy are all tied up. For SS16 they’ve started crisscrossing up the leg at your discretion, although the question isn’t how high you’ll try (the knee’s the lace-up limit for now)but whether you’ll knot, tuck, bow or leave them loose.
