WEAR NOW: SUMMER JACKETS

The essential outer layer

 

The ‘Goldilocks’ complex is real. You never want to be too hot or too cold in summer, just right, which a summer jacket can guarantee in changeable climates. Fashionable and functional, it has emerged as one of the savviest shapes around, a real wardrobe changer. Favoured fits like the bomber and blazer have simply lightened up (in colour and weight) and/or gained a whole host of exciting embellishments. Take your pick from the edit below.
