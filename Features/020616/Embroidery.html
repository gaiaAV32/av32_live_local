WEAR YOUR WANDERLUST

Five pieces by place

 

Rather than racking up airmiles, you can style your way around the world this season. Designers have referenced plenty of dream destinations in noteworthy collections that seem more freespirited than usual. From fluid flounces with hints of Havana to punchy Indian-inspired paisley; handcrafted styles by local artisans also double as authentic and ultra-stylish souvenirs. Here are five of our favourites.

 
 
 

Temperley London

Cuba is so hot right now and Temperley London travelled to Havana for SS16. The takeaway was a wonderful wave of wrap dresses, each one more vividly patterned than the last, with vivid pops of neon.

 

TEMPERLEY LONDON

Lime Printed Cotton Wrap Dress

 
 

Jérôme Dreyfuss

Jérôme Dreyfuss’ Left Bank polish goes via the Wild West this season. This fringed suede bag is pleasingly ordered thanks to the inclusion two interior pockets and a mini torch.

 

JÉRÔME DREYFUSS

Tan Goatskin Fringed Marion bag

 
 

Isabel Marant Étoile

Ikat prints take on a new insouciance under Isabel Marant Étoile. It’s the Parisian way, of course, to add inventive twists to make simple and relaxed styles really resonate. This easy mini is a case in point.

 

ISABEL MARANT ÉTOILE

Navy Cotton Shelley Wrap Skirt

 
 

SANCHITA

Multi Raffia Flatform Maarit Sandals

Sanchita

Strap on Sanchita’s flatforms to dip your toes into the colourful vibrancy of Bangalore. The eco-friendly brand is based there and uses out there adornments. Here, rhinestone and raffia take pride of place alongside luxury leathers.

 
 

Vanessa Bruno

The je ne sais quoi that Vanessa Bruno’s cropped jacket exudes seems aligned to the nature of the nomad. Bohemian stripes of lace and embroidered canavas bring a number of far flung climes [from Asia to South America] to&nsbs;mind.

 

VANESSA BRUNO

Indigo Cotton Printed Echem Jacket
