IN THE FRAME

Summer’s statement shades

 

There’s an inherent irony to sunglasses, in which lies their appeal. We put them on to protect, conceal, even hide behind and yet nothing ensures all eyes are you quite like covering yours with a pair of shades. Even the most unassuming styles have a whiff of mystery and drama to them. Now designers like Linda Farrow, Victoria Beckham and Yazbukey have further upped the eye-catching credentials of their sunglasses with daring details and cheeky touches. Welcome this summer’s most fabulous frames, which throw shade on the other accessories in your arsenal.
