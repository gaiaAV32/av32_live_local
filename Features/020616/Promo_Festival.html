FESTIVAL STYLE

Not a flower crown in sight

 

Flower crowns and an overly enthusiastic use of fringing just won’t cut it anymore. With so many new festivals joining the calendar, the definition of ‘festival fashion’ is evolving. We’ve put together some headlining looks to get you up to speed with the new agenda.

 

Music

BLK DNM’s classic jeans are more effortless than the obligatory denim cut-offs and leave more to the imagination. They foil the decorative pops of colour these versatile summer staples provide perfectly.

 

Literary

Channel the old school glamour of real and fictional characters with Lisa Marie Fernandez’s flirty dress. Topped with cool and elegant nude accessories, the stripes look even better with a tan.

 

Art

This is the perfect excuse to put your own wearable works of art on show. Cue Mira Mikati whose handpainted motifs playfully animate her designs and jeweller Charlotte Chesnais’ progressive metal forms.

 

Food

The delicious combination of bubblegum and butterscotch is enough to whet any appetite. Choose your accompaniments to taste; here, textured sandals and cut-out shades give the ‘60s inspired look a modern mentality.
