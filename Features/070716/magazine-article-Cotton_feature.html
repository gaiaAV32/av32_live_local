COTTON ON

Summer’s favourite fabric

 

Cotton conjures up all sorts of wonderful summer dressing possibilities. Soft and comfortable enough to make even an evening dress feel as effortless as a t-shirt, it’s also breathable, versatile and hypoallergenic. Tick, tick, tick! And, although we’ll always have space in our hearts – and wardrobes – for a freshly ironed, crisp shirt, or a super-soft, love-worn tee, this summer designers are experimenting with the classic fabric like never before.
