Isabel Marant continues to define the street style script with her sister line Étoile. The low-slung waists, relaxed cuts, flashes of trophy leather and the must-have accessories may share the same Parisian haute bohemian mood as her mainline but it’s that ‘I just threw this on and I look great’ vibe that makes Étoile truly seductive (not to mention a short cut to that muchimitated French insouciance).

 

Supplementing your own daily uniform with Isabel Marant Étoile’s Pre Fall 16 collection is a no brainer. This season you’ll find some of the coolest striped and patchworked knits around (complete with extra-long sleeves just begging to nonchalantly pushed up), which can be layered under tomboyish outerwear or over logoed t-shirts with slim trousers. Marant also makes a convincing case for the ruched mini skirt, alongside a wear-everywhere pleated midi and dainty lace blouse; while the standout, oversized denim jacket has ‘Endless’ day-to-night styling opportunities (literally) written all over it.
