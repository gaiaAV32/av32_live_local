Inspiration:
David Hockney

 

The art of Spring 17

A must-visit retrospective of British artist David Hockney’s 60 year career is currently on show at Tate Britain and if you cast your eye on fashion from the same period, you’ll appreciate just how influential his work is. Characterised by dramatic colour, provocative lines and endless technique experimentation, the 79 year-old has dabbled in multiple mediums including painting, photography and ipad drawing. Hockney himself is also quite the dandy, by certain accounts, partial to a pattern and an expert in colour-blocking. Here we highlight the Hockney-isms we’ve spotted from our designers this season.
