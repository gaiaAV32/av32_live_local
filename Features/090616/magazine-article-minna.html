FANTASY FOOTWEAR

Courtesy of Minna Parikka

 

 

Minna Parikka's bunny ear shoes instantly inspire smiles and involuntary coos of delight. It’s hard to get over how adorable they are. Parikka makes the cute ears cool (and just the right amount of kitsch) rather than gimmicky on her cult sneakers and loafers. Covered in bold prints, experimental textures, and peppered with pompoms, she also offers simpler styles to appeal to the minimalists who want to join the fun. Either way they’re utterly charming; the speed at which they’ve hopped into hearts and onto feet worldwide is testament to that.

Parikka’s self-confessed love of shoes and meticulous attention to detail shines through in her designs. She’s been bringing her footwear fantasies to life since launching her eponymous brand in 2005, after studying and working in London, Barcelona and Milan. With every playful pair designed to provide everyday entertainment, the premium materials and impeccable Spanish craftsmanship ensures comfort is never compromised.
