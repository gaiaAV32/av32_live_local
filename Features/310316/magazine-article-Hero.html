The COAT

Trenches don’t have to be beige and Litkovskaya’s lace version is the prettiest twist on the tradition. It can be a light layer, a delicate dress topper and much more.

The JUMPSUIT

Isa Arfen simplifies the chambray jumpsuit by bypassing buttons, removing sleeves and widening cropped legs. So far so chic but the belt, which looks best bowed, adds the brand’s witty charm.

The JACKET

The classic, collarless cut of Paskal’s jacket allows the surface circles to shine attracting admiring glances at every opportunity. They’re tempting to touch and sway playfully with every step.
