MEET MARTINIANO

Minimal shoes, maximum impact.

For simplified shoes that are excellently executed and incredible versatile, Martiniano's' your brand. Its failsafe flats sit comfortably among the chicest because they are essentially simplified ballet pumps and slippers elevated with the flexible grace and foot-moulding fit that dancers depend on. Designer Martiniano Lopez Crozet would know, given his performance art background, and every pair is hand-made in Argentina using only the finest and softest kid leather and time-honoured techniques. Embellishment free but richly coloured, these timeless styles command the same attention and admiration as bolder, busier styles.
