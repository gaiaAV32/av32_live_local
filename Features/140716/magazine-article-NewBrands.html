NEW BRANDS ON THE BLOCK

Four boho names to know

 

We’ve developed quite the knack for unearthing brilliant brands that will change your wardrobe for better. This season, Ulla Johnson, Sea NY, Muzungu Sisters and Talitha all make their debut on the Avenue. Judging by their eclectic prints and intricate embroidery, boho is back (albeit in a more poised, grown up guise). But don’t take our word for it. See for yourself with the new collections; no doubt you’ll want a piece – or several – of the action.

 

@ullajohnson: the designer herself

 
 

Ulla Johnson
New York designer Johnson has been weaving her global outlook into a relaxed yet urbane aesthetic since 2000. This season’s eclectic collection stars Czarist embroideries, Afghan embellishments and Japanese florals on feminine pieces often topped with a tassel or two.

Talitha
The brand the fashion pack always pack in their suitcase. Fashion editor Kim Hersov and Shon Randhawa have thoughtfully cultivated a haute hippy aesthetic with their free-spirited collections crafted by artisans in a New Delhi atelier. Dress the luxe bohemian pieces up or down with equal ease.

 

@muzunguSisters: Muzungu Sisters Pom-Pom Baskets

@muzunguSisters : Co-founder Dana Alikhani wearing the Jasmine dress

 

Muzungu Sisters
The word ‘muzungu’ means ‘traveller’ in Swahili, which gives you a hint as to this brand’s globetrotting credentials. Founders Dana Alikhani and Tatiana Santo Domingo ethically source the beautiful clothing and accessories on their travels, promoting and protecting traditional techniques.

 

Sea Ny Pre Fall 2016 Lookbook

Sea Ny
Monica Paolini and Sean Monahan’s New York based brand strikes a charming balance between uptown sophistication and downtown cool. Ready to be worn home and away, the timeless pieces feature tactile textures and prints which heighten their playful versatility.
