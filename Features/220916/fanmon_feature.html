Fanm Mon's embroidered dresses have a life beyond your vacation wardrobe

 
Here's a word for you to learn: vyshyvanka. Even if you can't pronounce that, you'll be familiar with them as the traditional Ukrainian embroidered dresses that have found their way onto the likes of Leandra Medine and Pandora Sykes in recent seasons. These ultra-pretty kaftan-style dresses may exude summer vibes, but we think Fanm Mon's versions are just too special to pack away when the sun's rays begin to lose their warmth. Their loose, flowing silhouettes and bold embroidery can work just as well in the city as they do poolside - it's just a simple matter of knowing how to style them for a different setting.
