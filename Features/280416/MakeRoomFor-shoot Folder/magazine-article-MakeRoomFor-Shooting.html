LEAVE ROOM FOR ROMANCE

SS16’s prevailing mood

Shakespearean heroine Ophelia is reborn, sartorially speaking, in a haze of unapologetic femininity. Delicate lace, blooming buds and sensual silhouettes take centre stage and everything is delightfully demure or tantalisingly translucent to add extra intrigue.
