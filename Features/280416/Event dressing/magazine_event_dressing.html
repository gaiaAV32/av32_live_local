GUEST STAR

How to shine at your next event

 

The greatest guest stars outshine the main cast or the other event attendees. If that’s your sartorial goal this Spring/Summer, we’ve got the scene-stealing looks to make it happen, whatever the dress code.

 

Garden Party

Take us literally with this starry lace midi (from Jonathan Saunders’ standout final collection), sexy silvery heels and an unexpected pop of green. These flattering shades and shapes are trans-seasonal so they’ll stun any time of year.

 
 

Wedding

You can stick to a pastel palette but use a poetic print to stand out in the inevitable sea of florals. This floaty, ruffled dress is just as romantic and artful accessories add a decorative dimension.

 

Summer Weekend

The easy, whimsical glamour of this bowed jumpsuit is perfect for weekends filled with sun, sea and impromptu soirees. Two tone flats and a mini trunk exude the same classic modern spirit and work wonderfully with it.

 
 

Match

Going to tennis/polo/ Ascot (delete as applicable) calls for a sense of free-spirited formality which this floaty top and lace pencil skirt combo nails. Heart vamp shoes and coloured rings are adorably directional additions.
