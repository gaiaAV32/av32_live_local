MOST WANTED SHOES

Stride through Pre Fall 16 in style

 

Like bags, shoes can and do have the ‘It’ factor. A dazzling detail or incredible fit that grabs attention, without compromising comfort of course, and elevates whatever they’re worn with. We’re pretty confident that our latest line-up ticks all these boxes and more, helping you find your footing for many stylish seasons ahead. From old favourites to bold newer brands, let us walk you through...

 
 

Sonia Rykiel

 

Step into the spotlight with Sonia Rykiel’s decedent and irreverent footwear. They may be dripping in glitter and gemstones, but they still have what chic, understated nonchalance that the French fashion so well.

 

Paul Andrew

 

Paul Andrew has crafted the ultimate day-to-night shoe. The Rhea is an impossibly elegant suede sling back, available in five shades that will complement everything in your wardrobe. Wear them all day, every day.

 
 

Tabitha Simmons

 

Stylist turned shoe designer Tabitha Simmons revamps staple styles with edgy glamour. Take the strappy, calfskimming Ginger heels, printed with assorted florals this season, or the statement seams of the Sibley Chelsea boot.

 

Martiniano

 

Martiniano’s minimal styles fit like a glove (and are named after them too) because the soft, supple leather moulds around the foot for a personal and superior fit. Enjoy new colours and styles like the Bootie.

 
 

Robert Clergerie

 

Robert Clergerie excels at exquisitely crafted boy-meet- girls shoes. With a few thoughtful twists, like the foot-framing curves of the backless loafers and the rich use of velvet, every pair switches tastefully between dresscodes.
