THE MAGIC OF MARCO DE VINCENZO

Introducing the brilliant new collection

 

One of the new guard of exciting designers shaking up Milan Fashion Week, Marco de Vincenzo is making waves with his wow-worthy womenswear. With the magpie eye of a maximalist backed up with precise craftsmanship, de Vincenzo blends experimental embellishments, lavish textures and vivid colours. The result? A modern take on glamour that also happens to be supremely wearable (just ask Beyoncé, who wore de Vincenzo’s red and white lace dress to the White House).

This season, de Vincenzo shows off his flair for creating clothes that are as pretty as they are punchy. Wide-legged, pleated trousers are taken to a bold new (and surprisingly flattering) level in rainbow plaid, while failsafe fits like the slinky slip dress or the wear-everywhere turtleneck are swathed in lurex or sequin stripes. Expect the wool bomber embroidered with a squirrel to assume trophy status in your wardrobe this season.
