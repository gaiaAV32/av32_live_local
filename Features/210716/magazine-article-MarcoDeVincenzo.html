THE MAGIC OF MARCO DE VINCENZO

The co-founders take us behind the scenes

 

Of the exciting luxury brands reimagining modern Italian femininity, Marco de Vincenzo’s womenswear really has the wow factor. The Sicilian born designer is colourfully maximalist: masterfully combining artistic references, experimental embellishments and lavish textures to delightful effect. None of the glamour or, most importantly, wearabilty is lost; and just like Beyoncé found when she wore Marco de Vincenzo to the White House, you’ll easily be the best dressed in a well-heeled crowd.

Never predictable but always covetable, the former Fendi designer’s Pre Fall 16 collection is exactly what your wardrobe has been waiting for. Trousers are taken to a bold new and surprisingly flattering level with progressive pleat placement, while failsafe fits like the slinky slip dress or the wear-everywhere turtleneck are peppered with streamlining matte and sequinned stripes. There’s even an adorable squirrel stationed on the back of a fringed bomber, ready to assume trophy and staple status in your wardrobe like everything else.
