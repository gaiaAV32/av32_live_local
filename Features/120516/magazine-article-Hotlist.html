With Jane Birkin in mind, Bower taps into the sportier side of her sex appeal. Models may be minimal but the higher cut briefs, low fronts and backs expose skin in a mesmerising way using two straps or less.

 
 

Zimmermann have managed to squeeze in all the most romantic details- lace, ruffles and florals- to feminise their strong 50s inspired shapes. As super cute and as low cut as they are, the brand’s carefree sophistication isn’t lost.

 
 

Lazul deliberates the details to set its stunning styles apart. Hardware is bespoke and braided, the wiring invisible, and the high performance material feels ultra-luxe. The tan-popping tropical tones and prints take them up another glamourous notch.

 
 

Prism focuses on framing your favourite parts of your figure using a range of techniques. The high-waisted briefs whittle while they work, the U-shaped wiring around the bust make a very supportive statement and the geometric print looks great against every skin tone.

 
 

Roksanda aka the Queen of Colour, always combines shades in wonderful ways you’ve never considered before and the wine/cobalt/black/pink/orange she chose for her newest swimwear is a case in point. Sculpted shapes are another one of her hallmarks that have chicly crossed over from her RTW collections.
