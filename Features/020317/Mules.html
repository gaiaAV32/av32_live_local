MUST HAVE MULES

Slip on and go

 
 
 

Fashion loves a comeback kid. Take the mule, it wasn't so long ago the much maligned shoe was destined to a life neglected at the back of wardrobes worldwide. Then - boom! - with blink and you'll miss it speed, suddenly you found them clacking along the catwalks and on the feet of cool girls everywhere. We've spotlighted this season's standout styles below.

 
 

Robert Clergerie

 

Another boy-meets-girl triumph from Robert Clergerie, these bottle green, backless loafers which curve beautifully around the foot are impeccably crafted and meticulously detailed.

ROBERT CLERGERIE Teju Green Alicio Slip-On Loafers

 
 

AVEC MODERATION Unicorno Fur Slip On Slide

Avec Moderation

 

Proof that fluffy mules aren't just for the boudoir, Avec Moderation's faux fur slides make a playful accent to simple summer dresses.

 
 

Proenza Schouler

 

It's official, clogs are back. Your proof? Proenza Schouler, where the bohemian favourite is artfully elevated thanks the contrasting heel. (Coming Soon)

PROENZA SCHOULER Black Kit Clog Mules

 
 
 

Maryam Nassir Zadeh

 

You might not need a pair of orange suede mules but thanks to Maryam Nassir Zadeh they've just shot to the top of wishlists. Her modernist sandals in unexpected shades have a cult following.

MARYAM NASSIR ZADEH Coral Penelope Mule

 
 

NO21 Truffle Slip On Flat Bow Sandal

No.21

 

No.21 takes the decorative shoe bow from pretty to progressive by twisting layers of buttery leather. Easily dressed up and down, they make an unexpected alternative to heels come evening. (Coming Soon)

 
 

3.1. PHILLIP LIM Espresso Louis Mule

3.1 Phillip Lim

 

3.1 Phillip Lim's chocolate pair fuses an equestrian aesthetic with an urban mentality. Toughened up with a two-tone front knot, wear them inside and outside of the city. (Coming Soon)
