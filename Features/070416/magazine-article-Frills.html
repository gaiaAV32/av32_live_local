The Frills

Subheading

Whatever you’re calling them – frills, ruffles or flounces – there is a difference between the season’s most desirable and feminine details. They’re everywhere: running across shoulders and framing sleeves and skirts, as delicate trims and cascading tiers. We narrowed it down, quite literally, to size: Frills are dainty, while ruffles tend to be larger and wider, meaning the flounce is the most dramatic of the bunch. However you decide to play it – delicate or daring, girly or graphic – we’ve picked some of our favourites to get you in the mood for this season’s most romantic trend.
