The Gentlewoman is a mysterious but familiar figure. Leaning towards quality over quantity and form, fabric and function before fast fashion, she’s the most discerning dresser among us. That’s not to say she doesn’t dabble in the more dramatic details or consider colour, it’s just that they have to be intelligently and elegantly engineered to last and slot seamlessly into her carefully curated wardrobe. Now the Gentlewoman has slipped into the limelight with this season’s streamlined and sculptural shapes, cutting a quiet but captivating figure. Watch and learn.
