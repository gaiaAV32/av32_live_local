LA CONTRIE Navy Blue Stitch Rohan Bag & Tan Fluro Pink Stitch Rohan Bag
 
La Contrie started off as a one woman passion project. Frustrated that she couldn’t find the ‘perfect’ bag – classic, well made, but not being toted by every other woman in Paris - founder Edwina de Charette de la Contrie chose to source some leather, learn to sew, and make her own. Fast forward five years, and with a team of five behind her, La Contrie’s workshop’s wares are cult-adored. Need any more persuading to get your hands on one? Find five (to run with the theme) reasons below.
