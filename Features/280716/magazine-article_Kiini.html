It used to be that when it came to swimwear, your choices were pretty limited: black or navy? Now, thanks to Turkish designer Ipek Irgit, all that’s changed. As the force behind game-changing label, Kiini, she’s catapulted bikinis and swimming costumes to it-status and gathered a cool cult following along the way (that’ll be the likes of Margot Robbie, Cara Delevingne and Leandra Medine). With their ultra-flattering fits and those signature colourful cotton crochet trims, expect to see the stylish swimwear on the girls having the most fun this summer. See the new collection here.
