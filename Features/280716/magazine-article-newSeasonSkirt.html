NEW SEASON SKIRTS

All the glory details

 

If you invest in one piece for Pre Fall 16, then make it a statement skirt. And by statement we mean splashed with brilliant prints, starring unexpected textures or peppered with irreverent details (sometimes all at once). The best bit? These minis, midis and maxis are also extremely wearable.

 



 

MACGRAW Blue Silk Horse Print Dressage Skirt

Have fun with your workwear in Macgraw’s full, fluid midi. The bold carousel horse print and palette ensures you’ll stand out in a sea of monochrome and neutrals.

 

 



 

ANNA K Navy Tartan Check Bow Skirt

Bows are an Anna KAnna K signature, here adding a directional trim to a modern kilt. Fitted, then flared, with just a flash of thigh, the punkish plaid also subverts their prettiness.

 

 



 

LITKOVSKAYA Green Frayed Tropic Midi Skirt

Thanks to the flattering A-line cut, frayed edges unveil an elegant demeanour on Litkovskaya’s artsy midi. Neutral accessories highlight the shimmering finish.

 



 

ISABEL MARANT ÉTOILE Black Leather Ruched July Mini Skirt

Swap your leather trousers for Isabel Marant Étoile’s trans-seasonal mini. Wear now with sneakers and a tee and just add a chunky knit and tights when the temperature drops.

 



 

ROSETTA GETTY Colour Block Satin Column Skirt

Showing off her command for pared-back luxury, Rosetta Getty makes a case for the evening skirt with this satin floor-sweeper. Swathed in bold stripes, it’s the modernist’s take on glamour.
