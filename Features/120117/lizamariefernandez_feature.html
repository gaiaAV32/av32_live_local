LISA MARIE FERNANDEZ

Want to know the secret to your favourite fashion girl’s beach style success? Lisa Marie Fernandez. The stylist-turned-designer outdoes herself every season by delivering the glamorous luxury swimwear and resortwear that streetstyle stars Olivia Palermo, Giovanna Battaglia Engelbert and Man Repeller’s Leandra Medine swear by.

Revamping sporty neoprene- Fernandez’s signature fabric along with waterproof denim- using on-trend colours and details, her body skimming, waist-defining pieces create the perfect pin-up silhouette. Before joining the #LMFgirlsonholiday, it’s worth noting that Resort 17’s key styles are the Yasmin and the Mira, named after Fernandez’s fashionable friends and fans.
