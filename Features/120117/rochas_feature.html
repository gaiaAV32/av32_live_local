REASONS TO LOVE ROCHAS

 
A close up of the Full Skirt Duchesse Gown
 
We’re seeing Rochas in a whole new light now that the French fashion house is debuting on the Avenue this season. Parisian couturier Marcel Rochas launched his eponymous brand, an ultra- feminine antithesis to Coco Chanel’s ‘la garçonne’ look, in 1925 and current Creative Director Alessandro Dell’Acqua is keeping the brand’s whimsical spirit alive with fresh impetus. Find five reasons why you should revel in Rochas’ re-emergence below.
