LONDON FASHION WEEK

The Big Apple’s leading lights

You’re always spoilt for choice in the creative melting pot that is London because as experimental as the designers based here are, they instinctively fill previously unseen wardrobe gaps and elevate your go-to silhouettes. Cue the global acclaim.

 
 
 
 

Natasha Zinko

Only Natasha Zinko could have transitioned from lawyer to jeweller to streetstyle star with a luxury womenswear atelier, impressive crown of red curls and an addictive instagram feed. Without taking herself too seriously, the Ukrainian’s quirky glamour and couture-like craftsmanship is bringing the fun back to cocktail hour dressing. Beyoncé’s on board.

Preen by Thornton Bregazzi

Remember the timeless-with-a-twist red evening dress that the Duchess of Cambridge wore on the Canada Royal tour last year? That was Preen and it underlines why British founders and spouses Justin Thornton and Thea Bregazzi have been in booming business for 20 years. Flawless fits, extra-special details and a hint of Victoriana.

Roksanda

Spotted on school runs, First Ladies and red carpets alike, Roksanda’s award-winning brand of luxury femininity revolves around dynamic day-to-night dresses and a rainbow of compelling colour combinations. The Central Saint Martins grad and selfprofessed ‘girly girl’ expresses her architecture background and love of modern art with sculptural embellishments.
