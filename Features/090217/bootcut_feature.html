BOOTCUT TROUSERS ARE BACK

Don’t let the name put you off because ‘bootcut’ is simply another term for ‘flared’. Specifically cut to make legs appear slimmer, these trousers get wider below the knee to frame or fall over your footwear. So brush aside memories of the low-slung, ill-fitting styles Britney Spears popularised in the early Noughties and embrace the most universally flattering trouser fit around.
