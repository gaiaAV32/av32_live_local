Immersing yourself, and your wardrobe, in some of the world’s most beautiful cultures has never been easier or chicer thanks to Muzungu Sisters. Founders Tatiana Santo Domingo and Dana Alikhani have made it their mission to prove that, contrary to popular belief, producing luxury fashion ethically and authentically doesn’t dampen desirability. If anything, knowing that local artisans handcrafted the gorgeous baskets bags and intricately embroidered clothing that the duo source on their travels makes them even more so.

The brand name ‘Muzungu’, translated as ‘traveller’ or ‘wanderer’ in Swahili, reflects Santo Domingo and Alikhani’s eclectic wanderlust and they ensure that makers are treated respectfully and paid for their work. Here, the friends reveal how they’ll be wearing their collection this summer. Feel free to take notes...
