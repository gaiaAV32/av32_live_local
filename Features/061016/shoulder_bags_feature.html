SHOW-OFF SHOULDER BAGS

 

Brother Vellies headlines our edit

 
 
Meet the styles that promise to banish bag boredom this season. We’re calling them the show-off shoulder bags, styles that are ultra-easy to wear but with the kind of bold, decorative details that deserve a double take. Leading the pack is critics’ darling Aurora James, the force behind hot Brooklyn-based name-to-know, Brother Vellies. James has scooped awards (including 2015’s CFDA/Vogue Fashion Fund AKA a sign of very big things to come) and lashings of acclaim with her hip accessories collection. And the best bit is that all materials used in Brother Vellies collections are sustainably sourced in Africa and handcrafted by local artisans. Must-have designs with a social conscience? Sounds good to us. We zoom in on a few of our favourites.
