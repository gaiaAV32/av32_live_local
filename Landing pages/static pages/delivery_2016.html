TRACKING MY ORDER

Once your payment has been processed you will receive an email informing you that your order has been shipped - also containing a tracking number which you can use to track your order on the relevant service. If using one of the DHL services (in the UK & EU) you will also receive a text message and email specifying your delivery time as well as giving you options for redirecting your delivery.

UK DELIVERY OPTIONS

When processing your order you will receive the option to choose from a variety of delivery options above. Please note that all delivery times quoted are from point of payment authorisation.

INSURANCE

All Orders and Returns are insured by Avenue 32 for loss or theft in transit up to the point of receipt. Please note that if you choose to use your own returns service; insurance of the parcel becomes your responsibility.

SHIPPING TO THE RUSSIAN FEDERATION
