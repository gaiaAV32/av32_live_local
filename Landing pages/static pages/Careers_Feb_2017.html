FASHION FEATURES EDITOR

We are looking for an enthusiastic & highly skilled individual to fill the role of Fashion Features Editor within our editorial team.

The role will report to the Creative & Editorial Director and will be integral to providing and managing the fashion features and written content for the Avenue 32 Magazine and all other creative written content. The ideal candidate would need to work well in a fast paced environment and have a strong background and comprehensive knowledge of fashion, fashion brands, runway trends as well as exceptionally strong writing and editing skills. The role also requires strong visual sensibilities.

Responsibilities:

• Generate editorial concepts liaising with the Creative & Editorial Director based on the requirements of other departments.

• Writing, editing and conceiving all editorial written content. Providing dynamic and enticing cover lines, headlines, sells and fashion stories to ensure maximum engagement.

• Managing & creating the editorial calendar. Seasonally as well as week on week.

• Lead ideas meetings with the writing & editorial team and develop editorial content that is both inspiring and meets the commercial requirements of the business.

• Liaise with external contacts and PR’s to secure interviews, acquire quotes when relevant.

• Managing, editing and developing the features assistant and any interns.

• Assist in checking all the weekly issues for errors and house style.

• Working closely with Creative & Editorial Director in order to ensure the tone meets brand requirements and reflects the imagery within the features.

• Liaising with other departments within the company to ensure the editorial department are meeting all written word requests.

• Staying up to date on fashion, style, influencers and the creative industry as a whole in order to coincide editorial features with social & commerce calendars.

• Tracking all stock movement, orders, quantities to ensure brands are being optimised

Skills and Experience:

• Experience in generating editorial content and story ideas for a leading fashion magazine or online magazine

• You will have a keen eye for trends and be confident in generating and delivering ideas

• Excellent written English and precise editing skills

• Time keeping and ability to produce a high volume of stories whilst meeting deadlines

• Expert knowledge of fashion, designers and brands

• Originality, creativity, and overall quality of editorial content

• Team Player with a resourceful can do approach, who thrives in an entrepreneurial, fast paced environment

• Confident in managing and developing junior members of the team

• At least 2 – 3 years experience in journalism with a large amount of experience in digital and with a proven background in fashion

Apply with a detailed cover letter and CV to careers@avenue32.com with your name and the role you are applying for in the subject line.
